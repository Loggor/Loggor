Plogr

Video sharing for musicians looking for honest feedback.

Welcome to Plogr.

Plogr is a site designed by musicians, for musicians. It will serve as a place for us to connect and share short videos of things we've been working on, and get feedback from other musicians.

upcoming features

The planned features that define this site are:

  • Video upload and viewing, so we can see what everybody's working on.
  • Profile pages and bios
  • A streamlined critique and rating system.
